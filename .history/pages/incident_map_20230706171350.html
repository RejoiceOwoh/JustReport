INCIDENT MAP

Welcome to the Incident Map, where you can explore reported incidents on campus. Use the interactive map below to view incident locations and gather information about specific incidents.

Reporting Instructions

Start by accessing the "Report Form" by clicking the button below. This will take you to a dedicated page where you can provide the necessary information.

Fill out the form with accurate and detailed information about the incident you are reporting. Provide the requested details, including the date, time, location, and description of the incident.

If available, attach any supporting evidence, such as photos, videos, or documents, that can assist in the investigation. Follow the provided instructions for attaching files securely.

Confidentiality and Privacy

We understand the sensitivity of the information you are sharing with us. Rest assured that your personal information will be treated with the utmost confidentiality. It will only be accessed by authorized personnel involved in the investigation. Your identity will remain protected, even if you choose to report anonymously.

Emergency Situations

If you are currently in immediate danger or witnessing an emergency, please contact the appropriate emergency services or campus security right away. Use our online reporting system after ensuring your safety and well-being.

Disclaimer
